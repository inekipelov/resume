# Alliance Digital iOS Developer Design

## Goal

Prepare a vacancy-specific application package for Alliance Digital that includes a tailored English iOS resume, a Ukrainian cover letter, and the required recruiter and technical interview question lists.

## Scope

Create and update only these files:

- `resumes/2026/ios-developer/en/alliance-digital-ios-developer/resume.md`
- `resumes/2026/ios-developer/en/alliance-digital-ios-developer/cover-letter-uk.md`
- `resumes/2026/ios-developer/en/alliance-digital-ios-developer/hr-questions.md`
- `resumes/2026/ios-developer/en/alliance-digital-ios-developer/technical-team-ceo-questions.md`

Also record the process artifacts:

- `docs/superpowers/specs/2026-04-15-alliance-digital-ios-developer-design.md`
- `docs/superpowers/plans/2026-04-15-alliance-digital-ios-developer.md`

## Source of Truth

- Use `contacts.md` for identity, contact data, location, language, and work context.
- Use `experience.md` for all experience, skills, projects, and education facts.
- Use the vacancy text for emphasis, ordering, and tone.
- Do not invent direct banking tenure, missing tools, or unsupported domain claims.

## Editorial Strategy

- Reuse the existing English iOS base resume as the structural baseline.
- Tailor the summary and experience bullets toward mature mobile product work, security-sensitive flows, UIKit plus SwiftUI delivery, architecture patterns, and release discipline.
- Position adjacent domain fit honestly through insurance, authentication-heavy mobile flows, regulated-like product constraints, and App Store delivery.
- Keep the cover letter warm, interested, and easy for HR to forward internally.
- Avoid repeating resume bullets inside the cover letter.

## Required Output Rules

- Keep the derived resume concise and factually strict.
- Keep the cover letter in Ukrainian.
- Create both checklist files next to `resume.md`, because this variant is not `base`.
- Do not update `README.md`, because no `base` resume path changes.

## Verification

Before publishing:

- Re-read every new file against `contacts.md`, `experience.md`, and the vacancy text.
- Check that the cover letter does not restate the resume section-by-section.
- Check that the question lists help qualify compensation, process, domain expectations, product ownership, and team setup.
