# HR Questions Checklist (Product Software Developer)

## Role Setup

- Is this role focused on product development ownership or task-based feature delivery?
- What is the expected split between hands-on coding, technical leadership, and cross-functional product collaboration?
- Who is the direct manager, and how is success evaluated in the first 3 and 6 months?

## Team and Collaboration

- What is the composition of the team across product, design, backend, mobile/web, and QA?
- How are product requirements documented and prioritized when scope changes mid-sprint?
- What level of autonomy is expected for technical decision-making and architecture choices?

## Process and Delivery

- What is the release cadence, and how are hotfixes and rollback decisions handled?
- Which quality gates are mandatory before release (tests, QA, monitoring, approvals)?
- Is there on-call responsibility, and what is the expected incident response model?

## Contract and Conditions

- Contract type, legal entity, payment schedule, and probation period details.
- PTO, sick leave, and public holiday policy for remote employees or contractors.
- Equipment policy and privacy boundaries for any monitoring or time-tracking requirements.

## Compensation and Growth

- Salary range for this role, currency, and review cycle.
- Bonus/equity policy and eligibility criteria.
- Learning budget, conference policy, and career progression path to senior/principal levels.
