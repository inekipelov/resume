# Technical Team / CEO Questions Checklist (Product Software Developer)

## Product Scope and Priorities

- What are the top product outcomes expected from this role in the next 90 days?
- Which user journeys or product areas are currently the highest engineering risk?
- How does leadership prioritize speed of delivery versus long-term maintainability?

## Architecture and Technical Boundaries

- What are the current architecture constraints and known bottlenecks in the codebase?
- Which services, APIs, and data models are considered unstable or under redesign?
- What non-functional requirements are mandatory: performance targets, reliability SLAs, and security constraints?

## Engineering Process and Quality

- What is the expected definition of done for production changes?
- How are design docs, architecture decisions, and technical debt tracked?
- Which automated test layers are required (unit, integration, end-to-end), and what is currently missing?

## Product-Engineering Collaboration

- How are product decisions validated with data, and which metrics define feature success?
- At what stage are engineers expected to challenge or reshape product requirements?
- How are trade-offs handled when roadmap pressure conflicts with technical quality?

## Leadership and Strategy

- What technical capabilities are strategic for the company over the next 12 months?
- Which product risks are currently considered critical by CEO/CTO?
- Is the main expectation to optimize existing product architecture or build new platform capabilities?
