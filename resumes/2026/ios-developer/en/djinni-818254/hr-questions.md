# HR Questions Checklist (Applefy - Senior Security Engineer iOS Reverse Engineering)

## Role Setup

- Is the project long-term product work or a fixed-scope engagement?
- What is the expected distribution between reverse engineering, mobile development, and automation tooling?
- Who is the direct manager for this role, and how is performance evaluated?

## Team and Collaboration

- What is the team composition for security, mobile, backend, and QA?
- Is there a dedicated security lead or principal reviewer for analysis outcomes?
- How are requirements communicated when analysis goals change mid-sprint?

## Process and Delivery

- What is the release cadence for security improvements and detection updates?
- What level of documentation is expected for analysis findings and recommendations?
- Is there an on-call or incident-response duty connected to this position?

## Contract and Conditions

- Employment model details: contract type, legal entity, and payment schedule.
- PTO and paid sick leave policy for full-remote contractors/employees.
- Equipment policy and security requirements for work devices.

## Compensation

- Salary range for this role and currency.
- Bonus policy and performance review cycle.
- Any budget for learning, certifications, or conference participation.
