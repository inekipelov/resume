# Technical Team / CEO Questions Checklist (Applefy - Senior Security Engineer iOS Reverse Engineering)

## Scope and Boundaries

- Which iOS applications are in scope for analysis, and who owns each codebase?
- Are all reverse engineering activities limited to first-party authorized environments?
- What legal/compliance guardrails define acceptable analysis techniques?

## Technical Expectations

- Which analysis depth is expected first: protocol behavior mapping, auth/session flow analysis, or detection bypass resilience?
- What are the priority abuse scenarios to detect and mitigate in the next 3-6 months?
- What output format is required for findings: technical reports, reproducible scripts, detection rules, or architecture changes?

## Tooling and Infrastructure

- Which internal tooling already exists for dynamic analysis, traffic inspection, and automation?
- What is the current stack for logs and data analysis, and who maintains it?
- Are there staging/sandbox environments dedicated to high-risk security testing?

## Engineering Integration

- How are findings translated into backlog items and production hardening work?
- Who approves security-related changes in mobile and backend repositories?
- What is the expected collaboration model between this role and backend anti-abuse teams?

## Leadership and Product Direction

- What is leadership's success metric for this role after 90 days?
- Which product risks are currently considered the highest priority by CEO/CTO?
- Is the goal primarily hardening existing systems or building a long-term internal security analysis capability?
