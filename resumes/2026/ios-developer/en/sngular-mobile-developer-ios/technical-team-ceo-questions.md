# Technical Team / CEO Questions Checklist

- [ ] What product area and user base will this iOS role support first?
- [ ] What is the current architecture of the app: mostly MVVM, MVP, VIPER, or a mixed legacy setup?
- [ ] How far has the codebase moved toward SwiftUI versus UIKit, and where are the biggest migration or maintenance pressures?
- [ ] What is the current testing strategy across unit, integration, and UI tests?
- [ ] How mature is the CI/CD pipeline, and what parts of release validation are still manual?
- [ ] Who owns App Store releases today, and how are release readiness and hotfixes handled?
- [ ] How is API design collaboration handled between iOS and backend teams?
- [ ] What are the main quality or delivery problems the team wants this hire to help solve in the first 3-6 months?
- [ ] How is technical decision-making shared across the Apps Chapter, and how much autonomy does each product team have?
- [ ] What would strong impact from this role look like after the first 90 days?
