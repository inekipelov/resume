# Interview Questions Playbook

Canonical reusable question bank for screening and interview calls.

## Useful Questions for HR

1. What are the first 1-2 month expectations for this role?
2. How is the hiring process structured, and what are the timelines for each stage?
3. What team setup will I join, and who will be my direct manager?
4. What are the work format details: schedule, core hours, and overtime policy?
5. What engagement model is available for my location (Ukraine or Spain)?
6. What compensation range is planned for this role, and how is final leveling decided?
7. Is there a probation period, and what are the success criteria?
8. Is time tracking used, and which methods are required by the company?
9. Are there requirements for monitoring tools such as screenshots or mouse-activity tracking?
10. How are learning, mentoring, and professional development supported?

## Useful Questions for Technical Team

1. What are the top technical priorities for the next quarter?
2. What does the current architecture look like (services, data flow, key integrations)?
3. What backend stack is used in practice (frameworks, DB, queues, caching)?
4. How are code quality and standards enforced (reviews, linters, testing gates)?
5. How are releases organized (branching model, CI/CD, rollback strategy)?
6. How is incident handling organized (on-call, alerting, ownership)?
7. What are the main technical bottlenecks or debt areas right now?
8. How are tasks scoped and estimated, and how is ownership distributed in the team?
9. What is expected from a new engineer in the first month?
10. Which engineering improvements would have the highest impact in this role?
